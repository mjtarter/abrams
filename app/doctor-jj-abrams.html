{% image jj-abrams.jpg %}

Dr. JJ Abrams joined Abrams EyeCare Associates in September 2004 after having a solo practice on the west side of Indianapolis since 1950. Dr. Abrams received his degree from the Northern Illinois College of Optometry. He is a fellow of the American Academy of Optometry and makes annual volunteer eye care missions to Central America. He is an officer of the Board of Directors of V.O.S.H., Volunteer Optometric Service to Humanity, and a member of the Midtown Indianapolis Exchange Club, a service organization which benefits inner city youth. He is a past president of Prevent Blindness, Indiana and the Southwest Health Center.
