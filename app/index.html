Caring for our Community

Abrams EyeCare recently participated at the "Through the Looking Glass" Gala fundraiser for the IN School for the Blind and donated a "SmartBoard" to the school. SmartBoards visual technology helps make images larger and more effective. Anything that is on a computer can be displayed on the SmartBoard, and printed into braille. SmartBoards have a suite of tools for teachers to enhance classroom learning and interaction. These SmartBoards get students up and out of their seats, interacting on the SmartBoard and with other students.
